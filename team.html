Our Passionate Team

At Hair Lab SLC, we're proud to have assembled a team of some of the most talented and experienced stylists in Salt Lake City. Each member of our team brings unique skills, creativity, and a genuine passion for helping you look and feel your best.

Lien

Master Stylist

Experience: 20+ years

Specialties: Precision Cuts, Color Correction, Asian Hair Textures

Lien has been creating stunning transformations since 2004, building a loyal clientele that follows her wherever she goes. Her precision cutting techniques and personalized approach ensure that every client leaves feeling confident and beautiful. Known for her attention to detail and warm personality, Lien has clients who travel from out of state just to have her work her magic.

"I love helping people discover their best look. It's not just about the haircut – it's about understanding each person's unique style and lifestyle."

Twan

Precision Cut Specialist

Experience: 15+ years

Specialties: Men's Cuts, Fade Techniques, Texture Work

Renowned for his meticulous attention to detail, Twan ensures every haircut is perfectly executed. His patient approach and dedication to his craft have earned him a reputation as one of the best stylists in the area. Twan takes the time to understand exactly what you want and delivers results that exceed expectations every time.

"Every haircut is an opportunity to create something perfect. I take my time because your hair deserves it."

Elena

Color & Style Expert

Experience: 12+ years

Specialties: Blonde Color, Asymmetrical Bobs, Modern Styles

Elena specializes in creating gorgeous blonde colors and cutting-edge asymmetrical styles. Her excellent communication skills ensure that you're involved in every step of the process, and her creative eye helps bring your vision to life. Elena's warm personality and professional expertise make every appointment a pleasure.

"Communication is key. I want to make sure we're on the same page so you leave absolutely loving your hair."
