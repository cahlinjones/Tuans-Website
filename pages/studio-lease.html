A Place to Grow Your Business

Are you a talented stylist looking for a professional space to call your own? Hair Lab SLC offers beautiful studio suites in the heart of Salt Lake City, designed to help you build and grow your business in a supportive, collaborative environment.

Our modern facility combines the independence of running your own business with the benefits of being part of a professional salon community.

What We Offer

Individual Studio Suites

Each suite is designed for your success with professional equipment, storage, and a welcoming atmosphere for your clients.

Great Views

Enjoy natural light and beautiful views that create a pleasant, uplifting environment for you and your clients.

Professional Amenities

Modern facilities with professional-grade equipment, comfortable waiting areas, and all the amenities you need.

Prime Location

Conveniently located in Salt Lake City with easy access and ample parking for your clients.

Supportive Community

Join a team of talented stylists who support and inspire each other while maintaining their independence.

Flexible Options

We offer flexible lease terms and options to suit your business needs and schedule.

Why Choose Hair Lab SLC?

Independence Meets Community

Run your own business while enjoying the support and camaraderie of fellow professionals. Share ideas, techniques, and success stories in a collaborative environment.

Professional Growth

Being part of Hair Lab SLC means access to continued education opportunities, product knowledge, and the chance to work alongside some of the best stylists in the area.

Build Your Brand

With your own studio space, you have the freedom to create your unique brand and client experience while benefiting from the established reputation of Hair Lab SLC.

All-Inclusive Amenities

Your lease includes utilities, WiFi, professional product dispensary access, laundry facilities, and more. Focus on your clients, not the logistics.

Who We're Looking For

We're seeking talented, professional stylists who:

  • Hold a valid cosmetology license in Utah
  • Have a passion for their craft and commitment to excellence
  • Value professionalism and client satisfaction
  • Are interested in building their own client base
  • Want to be part of a supportive salon community
  • Are self-motivated and entrepreneurial

Whether you're an established stylist looking for a new home or ready to take the next step in your career, we'd love to hear from you.
