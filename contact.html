Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, we work by appointment only to ensure each client receives our full attention and the best possible service. Walk-ins may be accommodated based on availability.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Just bring yourself! If you have inspiration photos or specific ideas, feel free to bring those along to share with your stylist during your consultation.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekend appointments or specific stylists. However, we often have same-week availability!

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time to other clients.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary consultations where we can discuss your hair goals, assess your hair's condition, and create a personalized plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and debit cards for your convenience.
